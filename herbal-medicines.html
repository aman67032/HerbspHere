Safe Use of Herbal Medicines

  • Always properly identify herbs before use
  • Research potential herb-drug interactions
  • Start with small amounts to test for allergic reactions
  • Consult healthcare providers if pregnant, nursing, or on medication
  • Store preparations properly to prevent contamination
  • Use quality ingredients from reputable sources
