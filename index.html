Frequently Asked Questions

What is HerbspHere?

HerbspHere is an interactive virtual herbal garden that helps users explore, learn, and engage with medicinal plants used in AYUSH. It offers a smart plant library, themed virtual gardens, and a gamified "My Garden" experience.

Who can use HerbspHere?

HerbspHere is designed for students, researchers, herbal enthusiasts, and anyone interested in learning about medicinal plants. You don't need prior knowledge—our platform makes learning easy and interactive!

Is HerbspHere free to use?

Yes! HerbspHere is completely free to explore, with future plans to introduce premium features for advanced learning and expert consultations.

How does the Virtual Herbal Garden work?

The Virtual Herbal Garden is a themed digital space where plants are categorized based on their medicinal properties (e.g., immunity boosters, skincare herbs). Users can explore, hover, and click on plants to learn about their benefits.

Can I create my own garden?

Yes! We are currently working on the "My Garden" feature which will allow users to customize and grow their own virtual collection of medicinal plants.

Is My Garden a real gardening guide?

While "My Garden" will be a virtual experience, it will also provide real gardening tips to help users grow medicinal plants at home.

How can I find a specific plant?

Use our Smart Plant Library with advanced search filters: by name (Scientific, Common, or Regional), medicinal uses, plant part used, or region/climate suitability.

Do I need to install anything?

No! HerbspHere is a fully web-based platform that works without installation.

Will you add more plants and features?

Absolutely! We're constantly expanding our plant database and adding new features like AI-powered plant recognition.

How can I give feedback?

Use the Feedback & Support section on our website to share your thoughts or report any issues.

Will there be an AI plant identification tool?

We're working on integrating an AI-based plant identification system for instant plant details from images.

Can I contribute plant data?

Future plans include a user contribution feature for suggesting plants or providing verified information.

Is there a community forum?

We're working on adding a community forum for discussions about medicinal plants and herbal remedies.

Does HerbspHere provide expert advice?

We're exploring partnerships with AYUSH practitioners to offer verified insights in the future.

Will HerbspHere remain free?

Core features will always be free. Premium features may include personalized gardening advice and expert consultations.

How do I get started?

Simply visit our website, sign up, and browse the Plant Library or explore the Virtual Garden.

How to search plants quickly?

Use the advanced search bar in the Plant Library with multiple filter options.

Can I bookmark plants?

Coming soon! A bookmark feature will let you save favorite plants for easy access.

Virtual Garden vs My Garden?

• Virtual Garden: Pre-built themed gardens • My Garden: Personalized space to grow your own plant collection

How accurate is the information?

All data is sourced from authentic AYUSH guidelines and verified botanical research.

Can I learn home gardening?

Yes! Each plant profile includes gardening tips about soil, water, sunlight, and seasonal care.

Are there step-by-step guides?

Yes! Our website includes guided tutorials for growing common medicinal plants.

How to report issues?

Use the Feedback & Support section to report bugs or suggest improvements.
